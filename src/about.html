  • Our Vision:

    is for everyone to have access to books that will enrich, improve, and change their lives by elevating their understanding of the world.

  • Our Mission:

    is to provide people all over our beloved country who do not have access to books with the opportunity to read for learning, enjoyment, study, and lifelong learning.

  • What we believe in:

    People gain power from books. They educate and inspire people to work toward a more equitable future. We have three different locations for our stores. Visit whichever is closest to you.

  • How we give back:

    Across Ethiopia, for every book purchased by you, we give free books to local community and public schools.
